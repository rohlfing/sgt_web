Graph Spectra

Draw a graph and then click 'Get Spectrum' to see the spectrum of the selected matrix. Click on an empty section of the canvas to create a vertex. Click two vertices to create an edge. Ctrl+click on a vertex will remove a vertex and all incident edges. Ctrl+click near teh midpoint of an edge will remove the edge.
